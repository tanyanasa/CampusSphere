Examination Cell

The Examination Cell of CampusSphere University is responsible for the smooth conduct of all examinations in accordance with university rules and academic regulations.

Functions of Examination Cell

  • Conduct of Semester and Annual Examinations
  • Issue of Admit Cards and Examination Schedules
  • Evaluation and Declaration of Results
  • Revaluation and Supplementary Examinations

Important Information

  • Students must carry valid ID cards during examinations
  • Examination rules must be strictly followed
  • Results are published on the official website

The Examination Cell ensures transparency, fairness, and timely completion of all examination-related processes.
