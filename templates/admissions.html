Admissions

Programs Offered

  • Postgraduate Programs
  • Diploma and Certificate Courses

Admission Process

  • Final Admission Confirmation

Eligibility

  • Minimum qualification as per program requirements
  • Fulfillment of university admission criteria

Applicants are advised to regularly check official notifications for important dates, guidelines, and updates related to admissions.
